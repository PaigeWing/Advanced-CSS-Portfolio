About Me

Hello and welcome to my about me. My name is Paige. I live and grew up in Utah and absolutely love it! There is nothing quite like the scenery and the limitless exploration that can be done. Last year in Hawaii I married the one my heart loves. Between the two of us we have three beautiful kids (Jace, Summer, Claire) who make life exciting and very fulfilling. On top of three crazy kiddos, I have three crazy fur baby dogs. It is always an adventure at our house. Although I do not get a lot of free time, when I do I enjoy indoor rock climbing and going to see a good movie.

Just recently I decided to make a career change to web development. It has been scary but I am excited to see what the future holds and all its possibilities. I love the idea of creating and having that accomplished feeling after making something that I can call mine. It is also very important to me that my kids can see me chasing my dreams and going out of my comfort zone to get where I want in life.
